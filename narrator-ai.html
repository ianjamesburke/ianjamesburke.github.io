NarratorAI

An end-to-end AI production platform — turns scripts into fully produced videos, and gives the team a natural-language interface for Facebook ad analytics.

Next.js Firebase Gemini ElevenLabs Fal AI Facebook Graph API
Video Pipeline
Script-to-video in stages — scene splitting, character design, AI stills via Gemini, voiceover sync, and Ken Burns video assembly via Fal AI. Fire-and-forget with async status tracking.
AI Media Gen
Image and video generation with reference image support, style presets (Pixar 3D, claymation, graphic novel), and ElevenLabs TTS for custom voiceovers with voice selection.
Analytics Chat
Gemini-powered chat with custom Genkit tools — queries Facebook ad performance, runs account-level reports, compares time ranges, and surfaces top-performing creatives in plain English.
